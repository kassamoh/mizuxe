ABOUT

Natalia Foundation has been into existence for a Period of time and over the course of its existence it has implemented many projects with different implementation supporting partners. even now it has projects in place ready to be implemented according to organization`s strategic plan.

Natalia Foundation believes that investing in young people especially vulnerable children, adolescents, youth is an important strategy towards achieving the sustainable development in the community i.e. individually and community level.

Natalia Foundation partnered with me to create a new design from scratch and development of Natalia Foundation website and Google my business that represent their organization`s strategic plan and the goals they want to achieve to help the community.
