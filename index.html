My Web Design Process.

①

Consultation

consultation with a client to discuss about business goals or his/her clients client goal, needs and requirements to drive more clients to his or her business.

②

Content Strategies

I define how the content on the site is going to be constructed, by starting with content strategies is to show how a client business is communicating their values.

③

Visual Design

Then I create visual design, in visual design I create The website wireframes and Prototype that I will show a client on how the website will look like and work.

④

Design iteration

Review the prototype with a client and show how the website look and work. If the client don't like the design I will make changes until a client satisfied with it.

⑤

Build and test

I start build the website based on client final design, I build and test to make sure that the website will be responsive across all devices for better user experience.

⑥

Deploy

I deploy the client website on reliable and fast web hosting services that will make sure his or her website will be available online in 24 hours a day.
